INSTRUMENT OF PROGRESS

Eyetfu iSwati Organization envisions a nation where the economic strength, health and human rights of the most vulnerable groups and developing communities are actively realized, recorgnized and respected. Our mission is to mount a long-term effort against hunger and malnutrition through advocacy of financial literacy, information dissemination, legislative awareness, research, and direct service provision, thus achieving national economic freedom to Eswatini as a whole.

Economic Strengthening

In light of the status quo of Eswatini and its respectful counterpart, we are proceeding to engage under privileged communities in all constituencies with the aim of empowering them with economic incentives that will aid in establishing agrarian reforms that will incorporate beneficiaries with our programs. Our programs will serve the purpose of coordinating and implementing agrarian reforms with the idea of setting up strategically plan extensive primary production. We Emaswati as a people are agricultural, our culture has reflected such in numerous ways since time immemorial. It is therefore best that we strive to accomplish our utmost civilization through our available resources and cultural inclinations. Our moral vision is to have a moral compass that that shall be implemented in unison and incorporate all affiliated and non-affiliated individuals in the vision of realizing a civilization based of agrarian practice contemporary and custom to each environment. All existing civilization emerged from the practice of agriculture and with this in mind, our mandate is to utilize all vacant land in the country and to establish communities linked with a moral vision of progressively developing Eswatini through a coordinated movement of all Emaswati.

Youth Empowerment

Therefore, the value of the generation characterized with youth is of the utmost importance, the future of the country rests in their minds. If the goal is to generate and cultivate man and woman of worth with strong values and capable minds, current authorities and platforms must enable these individuals to be capacitated with these intents in mind through contemporary methods and an atmosphere of evolution. Eyetfu iSwati organization understands and appreciates the complexity of such a mandate and the intricate barriers that need demolition so to allow a generation devoid of inherited complexities, frustrations, fears and neurotics. It is why we deem it necessary to develop strategies that will be inclusive of individuals in these age groups and connect them with leaders who serve as examples of exceptional principles.

Goals

Eyetfu iSwati is committed to shape practices and influence policies in humanitarian and development settings in order to positively impact lives of people and their communities. Eyetfu iSwati aims to enable better and more effective decision making by generating and promoting knowledge, tools and practices for humanitarian and development stakeholders.
